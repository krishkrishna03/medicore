About Us

Transforming Healthcare Operations

MediCore HMS was built to eliminate paperwork and streamline workflows so your team can focus on what matters most — patient care.

HIPAA CompliantSecure & Private

Real-time Operations

Live dashboards for admins, doctors and patients — no refresh needed. Everything updates instantly.

Secure Role-Based Access

Admins, doctors and patients each access only what they need. JWT-protected with bcrypt encryption.

Automated Notifications

Doctors receive login credentials by email when onboarded. Patients get alerts for appointments and bills.

Works Everywhere

Fully responsive design that works seamlessly on desktop, tablet, and mobile devices.

Services

Everything Your Hospital Needs

A complete suite of tools to manage every aspect of your hospital in one place.

Patient Management

Register patients, manage medical records, allergies, blood groups and complete health history.

Smart Scheduling

Real-time slot availability, one-click appointment booking and automatic conflict prevention.

Doctor Portal

Doctors view their daily schedule, write prescriptions, add diagnoses and manage patient records.

Billing & Payments

Auto-generate consultation bills, track outstanding payments and process multiple payment methods.

Pharmacy Inventory

Track medicine stock levels, get low-stock alerts, manage pricing and restock with one click.

Analytics Dashboard

Visualize hospital performance — appointments, revenue trends, doctor workload at a glance.
